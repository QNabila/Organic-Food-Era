About Me

Hey y’all, welcome to Organic Food Era! I’m Nabila and I’m so glad
you’re here! In short: I like to make clean-eating food that is delicious,
good for you, and simple. I like big flavors without a lot of fuss or time.
I find the best way to maintain a healthy lifestyle is to LOVE what you
eat and how you go about your day, and I’m here to share that with you.
Here, you’ll find TONS of recipes, from sweet to savory, that are easy
to make, but oh-so-yummy. My goal is for you to eat the food you love
— just in a healthier way — and NOT go crazy in the process. That’s why
you’ll see a heavy focus on meal prep because it is a total life-saver if you
wanna eat healthy and still have a life.

Chimichurri Sauce

Compared to pesto, chimichurri has a bright taste that let’s the herbal flavors of cilantro and parsley shine strong. The taste is complimented nicely with a tangy lift of lime and the rich flavor of olive oil. You can adjust (or leave out) the amount of jalapeno depending on your tolerance for spicy food. I tend to find chimichurri sauces a little too strong when raw white onion is added. so I use a red onion. This adds more sweetness and less of a bite.

Tenderloin Steak

Ideally, you should not marinate a steak for more than 24 hours — especially if you have a tender cut of steak. Any longer than that and the muscle fibers will break down and the texture will become mushy.Some types of steak, like skirt steak, are naturally not very tender and should be left to marinate for a longer time in order to tenderize.
